Day 26: The "Best Friend" Recipe

Found this old recipe from 6th grade. I think it's still pretty accurate.

Ingredients:

Pour a cup of friends in a bottle. Pick your closest one. They are your best friend.

Instructions:

If they are frying, always ask if they need help. Drizzle them with love, and remind them they are your best bud. Sometimes you guys will steam, but humans make mistakes. Just let it rinse because you will forgive each other. Always in tough places you will need one another. Then just simmer, and you are done, you have a bowl with your best friend and they will not be gone.
